# AI Opportunity Assistant

MOJAD AI Assistant helps users discover opportunities.

Examples:

- Find jobs
- Find investors
- Find services
- Find grants
- Find suppliers
- Find startups
- Find education programs

Example commands:

"Find me a job in Zambia"

"Find investors for my startup"

"Show scholarships in Africa"

Purpose:

Reduce friction between people and opportunities.

Future:

Voice-powered multilingual assistant.
